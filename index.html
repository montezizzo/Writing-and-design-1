What is good design?

My mom, who studied industrial design at Pratt, told me one of the key tenants officia design is 'form follows function'. Most things we appreciate the design of look clean and interesting, but what makes a design truly good depends on how we interact with it. For instance, the Vanmoot bike which is first in the grid below looks very nice, but the built in battery, motor, and light are the factors that would make owning the bike a benefit. Good design is about both how the product looks and what it can do.
